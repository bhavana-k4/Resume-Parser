The two bugs that were causing the errors вҖ” the old `google.generativeai` package is fully deprecated and throws a `FutureWarning`, and `gemini-1.5-flash` is no longer available on the free API tier. The new SDK is `google-genai` (imported as `from google import genai`) and the model is now `gemini-2.0-flash` which is the latest, fastest, and free.
